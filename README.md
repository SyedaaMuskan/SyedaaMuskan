## 🧠 About Me

I'm an AI/ML Engineer building **multi-agent AI systems** and **GenAI-powered products** — the kind that don't just demo well, but actually ship. My work sits at the intersection of applied machine learning and intelligent system design, with a strong pull toward the **Microsoft Azure AI stack** (Azure OpenAI, Cosmos DB, Azure AI Search) and hackathon-speed product development.

- 🔭 Currently building **multi-agent architectures** that reason, orchestrate, and act — not just chat
- 🧩 Deep focus on **RAG pipelines, LLM orchestration, and predictive ML models** solving real-world problems
- 🏆 Hackathon builder — I take an idea from whiteboard to a working, demo-ready product fast
- 🌱 Fresher by title, not by output — my projects are built to production standards
- ⚡ Fun fact: I care as much about *why* a model predicts something as I do about *what* it predicts
